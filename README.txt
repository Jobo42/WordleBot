This program efficiently solves Wordle ("https://www.powerlanguage.co.uk/wordle/")
using an algo. that looks at the words with the most common letters in
their respective positions.

e.g.

Let's say the frequency that the letter "e" as the first letter in any five letter word is 10%,
the frequency for "a" as the second letter is 20%, "t" as the third later is 20%, 
"e" as the fourth letter is 15%, and "n" as the fifth and last letter is 10%.

The score for the word "eaten" woud be product of all the frequencies: 0.00006

Given what words/letters have been used already, we can come up with an educated guess
on what word we pick next will have the highest chance of being correct.
